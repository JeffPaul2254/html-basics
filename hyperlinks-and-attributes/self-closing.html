Jon Snow is a fictional character from George R.R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire book series and its television adaptation, Game of Thrones. Portrayed by Kit Harington, Jon is the 14-year-old illegitimate son of Eddard "Ned" Stark, Lord of Winterfell, and half-brother to Robb, Sansa, Arya, Bran, and Rickon Stark.
